Our Story

We don't sell homes. We place them.

Macan Homes was founded on a simple proposition: that the rooms we live in shape the lives we lead. Every residence we represent is studied, sometimes for years, before it ever reaches a brochure.

Our practice is small by design. We work with architects, gallerists, and a network of clients who understand that the right home is rarely the most listed one.

— The Macan Atelier

Services

A practice in four quiet movements.

01

Acquisition

Off-market sourcing, discreet negotiation, and architectural diligence — undertaken with the patience that distinguishes a residence from a listing.

02

Representation

Bespoke campaigns built around the architecture itself. Editorial photography, private viewings, and positioning that honors the work.

03

Curation

Interior commissions in collaboration with a vetted bench of designers, craftspeople, and ateliers in Milan, Kyoto, and Los Angeles.

04

Stewardship

Ongoing care of a residence after closing — from staffing to seasonal opening, art handling, and the slow work of becoming a home.
